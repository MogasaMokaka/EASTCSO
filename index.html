The EASTC Students Organization (EASTCSO) is the official student body of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre. We coordinate student activities, events, announcements, and communication between students and the EASTC administration.

Learn More Contact Us

About EASTCSO

The EASTC Students Organization was established to represent and serve the interests of all students at the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. We work closely with the administration to ensure a productive learning environment and an active campus life for every student.

Our Goals

  • Promote academic excellence and student welfare
  • Coordinate student clubs, sports and social events
  • Serve as the official bridge between students and EASTC administration
  • Foster leadership skills and professional development
  • Ensure fair and equal representation of all students
